Assignment4 Add Sound To Your Game

Add sound to your game

Create a sound manager as showed in class and add sound to your game.

You should at least:
Have one background music.
Have FOUR clips of sound effects.
Play at the correct time point.

Other resources you may also want to refer to:

http://docs.unity3d.com/Manual/PartSysWhatIs.html

You may try to use Particle Systems to make the visual effects. You can do this by instantiate prefabs of particle system at the position where something happens.

Be creative, and have fun with making your game more JUICY!
